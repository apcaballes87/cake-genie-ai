# Baptismal Guide Blog Post Plan

## Overview

Create a comprehensive blog post for Genie.ph about getting a child baptized in Metro Cebu, covering:

- List of churches with contact info and addresses
- Requirements and documents needed
- Step-by-step process
- Important considerations

## Content Structure

### 1. Introduction

- Brief explanation of baptism in Catholic churches
- Why this guide is helpful for parents in Cebu

### 2. Churches in Metro Cebu

Cover major churches in:

- **Cebu City**: Basilica Minore del Santo Niño, Metropolitan Cathedral, San Jose Parish, Sacred Heart Church, Divine Mercy Shrine, St. Francis of Assisi (Guadalupe), St. Joseph Parish (Mabolo)
- **Mandaue City**: St. Joseph Parish, St. Therese of the Child Jesus Parish
- **Lapu-Lapu City**: Mactan Parish (St. Augustine), Lapu-Lapu City Church
- **Talisay City**: St. Teresa of Avila Parish

### 3. General Requirements

- PSA Birth Certificate
- Marriage Certificate of parents (if married in church)
- Godparent requirements (1-2 godparents, Catholic, confirmed, at least 18 years old)
- Baptismal certificate from previous parish (if transferring)

### 4. Step-by-Step Process

1. Schedule appointment with church
2. Submit requirements
3. Attend baptismal seminar (if required)
4. Attend baptismal interview
5. Pay fees
6. Attend the baptism ceremony

### 5. Important Considerations

- Dress code for child and godparents
- What to bring on the day
- Typical fees
- Godparent responsibilities
- Photography rules

### 6. Tips and Final Thoughts

## Implementation

- Write content in markdown format matching Genie.ph blog style
- Include HTML image tags for potential images
- Add disclaimer about verifying information directly with churches
